Croissant

Description

Croissants are the buttery, flaky marvels of the pastry world, beloved for their delicate layers and rich aroma. Making them at home feels almost magicalâ€”simple dough, transformed through time, folding, and patience into something elegant and irresistible. Though the process takes effort, the reward is a warm, golden pastry that crackles slightly when touched and melts delightfully on the tongue. Whether enjoyed plain, filled, or with a cup of coffee, a well-made croissant delivers comfort and a little taste of French craftsmanship.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Egg (for egg wash)

Steps

  1. Make the dough and let it rest.
  2. Prepare the butter block.
  3. Wrap the butter in the dough.
  4. Roll and fold the dough to create layers.
  5. Shape the croissants.
  6. Proof and bake until golden.
